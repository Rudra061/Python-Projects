Title: Math Painter

Description: An app that lets the user provide the start coordinates of geometrical shapes such as squares and rectangles,
             their dimensions, their colors and the program produces an image file canvas with the geometrical shapes
             drawn in it

Classes:
            Square:
                X
                Y
                side
                color
                draw(canvas)

            Rectangle:
                X
                Y
                side
                color
                draw(canvas)

            canvas:
                width
                height
                color
                make(imagepath)
